SmartWrite

SmartWrite is a application currently in development with a group of 6 other students, we aim to create a tool to help students and professors alike in creating more organized and effective notes.

The application will use a combination of tools including handwriting recognition, AI writing assistance, and a comprehensive organization system to make the task of taking notes easier and more efficient.
